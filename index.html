Hey, I'm Thomas.

I grew up in Tampa and live in Austin. Both feel like home, which I think is a good thing to have.

My wife Jenny and I met in high school, lost track of each other, and reconnected years later through a mutual friend. We have a French Bulldog named Bru who is a goofy little pain in the ass.

My own path has been in operations and AI, but on weekends you'll find me running long distances with friends, practicing guitar, or reading. I'm drawn to people who take their craft seriously.

This site is where I think in public. I write about building things, fitness, deep work, and whatever else is on my mind. No brand. Just a person with a website. More about me →
