About Me

Hi, I'm Thomas.

I grew up in Brick, New Jersey, moved to Tampa as a kid, and ended up in Austin through work. I've been here a few years now and it's starting to feel like a second home. It has small-town energy, a real music scene, and people who are genuinely nice.

I'm married to Jenny. She's caring, empathetic, and the opposite of me in most ways that matter — she wants to be out doing things, I'm happy in my corner. It works. She drags me out when I need it.

Our French Bulldog Bru doesn't care about any of that. He just wants to lay between my legs and get attention, and he'll interrupt whatever I'm doing until he gets it.

My dad has a saying: "Life is simple. We make it hard." I come back to it more than any book I've read.

How I spend my time

I run marathons. I play guitar most days — I get into flow pretty quickly with it. It's relaxing, creative, and fun once you can play songs you like.

I'm also building things on weekends: tools, scripts, small projects (this website). Most go nowhere. That's fine. I enjoy it.

I read a lot. Marcus Aurelius, Cal Newport, Oliver Burkeman. You can see what I've been reading on the Books page.

What this site is

A space on the internet that I own. I use it to put down thoughts, document things that matter to me, and write about what I'm learning. Platforms come and go, this stays.

If you're looking for the professional side — AI work, projects, and background — that lives at thomas-denny-ai.gamma.site.
