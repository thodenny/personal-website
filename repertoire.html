  • Layla Eric Clapton
    Intermediate
    Blues Rock

    I love the history behind this song. The song was written by Eric Clapton for Pattie Boyd, who at the time was the wife of his close friend, George Harrison of The Beatles. Clapton had fallen deeply in love with her and was struggling with the emotional weight of his feelings for his best friend's wife.
