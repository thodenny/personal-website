Book Notes

The Tiger

John Vaillant  ·  2010  ·  8/10

A tiger in Siberia decides to hunt humans, methodically, over days. It's terrifying and the setting makes it worse. Vaillant writes the landscape so well you feel cold reading it. Moral of the story: don't mess with mother nature.

Passages I underlined and quotes that stuck. In the order I encountered them.
